Earning point rule

Earning point rule means that selected service of products will be given a specified point by the branch admins then if a loyal customer purchase those products then the point will be added to that customer then if the cumulated point is sufficient for the available reward then they can redeem the reward.

 

The other rule in this page will be the Sign up,Refer,Share in here if a new customer sign up to the system,if loyal customers refer others ,and share the content to other person respectively then points will be given to those customer according to the available points.
